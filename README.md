# customer-shopping-behaviour-eda

**Project Overview**
This project focuses on performing an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on shopping dataset. This helps to understand the shopping behaviour of the customers and their shopping preferences. Finally giving some recommendations that will help the business to attract more customers.

**Objective**
1. Understand and analysing the customer's shopping pattern.
2. Find the opportunities.
3. Give recommendation to increase the sale and attarct the customers.

**Dataset**
Source: Kaggle
Description: This dataset consists of columns like age, gender, their subscription status, products they purchased and so on.
Size: 18 columns, 3900 rows

**Tools and Technologies**
1. Colab
2. NumPy, Pandas
3. Seaborn, Matplotlib
4. Python
   
**Analysis Performed**
1. Understanding data and handling missing values.
2. Revenue generated across various locations.
3. Spending pattern across various age group
   
**Key Insights**
1. Customers in the age group 50 to 60 years tend to spend more.
2. The number of customers who have taken the subsciption is significantly low.
3. Male customers tend to spend more than female customers.
    
**Business Recommendations**
1. Giving special offes for the customers who have the subsciption will help to increase the number of subscribers, which is very less at present.
2. Understanding the market trends and demands to attract more of female customers as well.
3. Analyzing the type of products purchased by customers of various age group.
   
**Next Steps**
1. Develop forecasting model to analyse the trends and demand for products well in advance.
2. Identifying and selling the products that are purchassed together.
